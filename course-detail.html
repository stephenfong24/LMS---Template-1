Advanced UI Design Architecture

Master the geometric principles of high-scale design systems.

Course Overview

Lumina's Advanced UI Design Architecture path focuses on the systematic application of geometric balance in digital products. Students will move beyond "making things look good" and start building structural frameworks that support complex data and user flows. This course is specifically designed for intermediate designers looking to transition into Lead or Architect roles.

What you'll learn

Scaling design systems for enterprise
Geometric and structural grid logic
Advanced token management (JSON)
Verified accessibility audit patterns

Path Curriculum

Understanding the golden ratio, modular scales, and the mathematical foundations of spacing systems.

Building responsive frameworks that maintain proportion across ultra-wide and mobile viewports.
